SORTS:


Orders all restaurants by the price from low to high.

Here's the key:
$ ........... Under $10
$$ ................. $11-30
$$$ ............... $31-60
$$$$ ...... Above $60


Orders all restaurants by the ratings from highest to lowest. If ratings are the same, restaurants are ordered by the number of reviews.

Orders all restaurants by the name in alphabetical order (A-Z).

Orders all restaurants by the cuisine in alphabetical order (A-Z).

CARD TOOLS:


Generates a random restaurant for you to try!

Adds a restaurant of your choice.

The user will be prompted to type in all details of the restaurant (image URL and Yelp URL are optional). A card of the added restaurant will appear with the entered information.


Removes the restaurant of your choice.

The user will be prompted to type in the name of the restaurant to be removed.


Removes the last restaurant in the list.
